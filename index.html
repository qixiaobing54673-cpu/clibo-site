Questions

Is my clipboard data private?

Yes. Everything is stored in a local database on your Mac. There's no account, no cloud sync, and no analytics. Content marked sensitive by password managers is never recorded.

I already use Maccy. Can I switch easily?

Yes — Clibo imports your existing Maccy history in one click, and also reads JSON, CSV, TXT and Markdown files.

Is it a subscription?

No. Clibo is a one-time $1.99 purchase. Because your data and AI keys stay on your machine, our costs stay low — so you don't pay monthly.

What are the system requirements?

macOS 13 (Ventura) or later, Apple Silicon or Intel. The app is signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple.

Why isn't it on the Mac App Store?

Clibo uses a local on-device search engine that the App Store sandbox doesn't allow. Distributing directly also means no 30% cut — which helps keep the price at $1.99.
