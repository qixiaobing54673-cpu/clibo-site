Privacy promise

Last updated: June 2026

Everything stays on your Mac

Clibo stores your clipboard history in a local database inside your user Library folder. It is never uploaded anywhere. There is no Clibo account and no cloud service.

No telemetry, no tracking

Clibo does not collect analytics, usage data, or personal information. The app works fully offline. Crash logs, if any, are written locally and never sent automatically.

Sensitive content is respected

Content marked as concealed or transient by password managers (such as 1Password) is never recorded. You can also clear all history or exclude specific apps at any time.

Optional AI uses your own keys (BYOK)

When optional AI features are enabled, requests go directly from your Mac to the provider you configure (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, or a local Ollama) using your own API key. They never pass through any Clibo-hosted server.

Payments

License purchases are handled by our payment processor. Clibo only receives the license status needed to activate the app; we never see your full card details.
